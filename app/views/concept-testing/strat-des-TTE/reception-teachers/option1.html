Course modules

Module 1: Understanding child development and variation

This module covers:

  • The importance of early childhood education and care, including how to implement strategies to provide a positive approach in your setting
  • The importance of child development knowledge and the various factors that impact on children’s development
  • the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on children’s development

Module 2: Identifying pupils needs

This module covers:

  • Recognise how social and emotional development, language and communication proficiency may impact learning
  • Understand the importance of your role in supporting children’s individual differences and needs, creating an inclusive environment and building effective partnerships
  • Implementing formative assessments to identify individual starting points and plan next steps

Module 3: Supporting speech, language and communication

This module covers:

  • Identify and explore the stages of language development in the early year
  • Review the potential factors that can affect language development
  • Understand how to identify and support children with speech, language and communication needs (SLCN)
  • Explore how to develop responsive adult-child interactions to support learning

Module 4: Develop adaptive approaches to early writing

This module covers:

  • Understand the developmental journey towards writing in the early years
  • Explore inclusive strategies to support mark-making, fine motor skills, and composition
  • Address barriers to writing, including those linked to age, language, or SEND
  • Use assessment to celebrate progress and ensure all children feel successful

Module 5: Working with families to support child development

This module covers:

  • Build strong, respectful relationships with families to support development and safety
  • Collaborate with families to identify and respond to individual needs
  • Promote a shared commitment to safeguarding and inclusive values across home and school
